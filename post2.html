Semiya payasam, Vermicelli Kheer

Semiya Payasam, also known as Vermicelli Kheer, is a beloved dessert in Andhra Pradesh that graces every festive occasion and special gathering. This creamy and luscious sweet dish is made by simmering roasted vermicelli in milk, sweetened with sugar, and flavored with aromatic cardamom. The addition of cashews and raisins sautéed in ghee adds a delightful crunch and richness to the kheer.
Often prepared during festivals like Ugadi, Diwali, and special family celebrations, Semiya Payasam is not just a dessert but a symbol of love and togetherness. The simplicity of its ingredients, combined with its comforting sweetness, makes it a favorite among all age groups. Whether served warm or chilled, Semiya Payasam brings a touch of indulgence and tradition to the dining table, making every occasion memorable..
